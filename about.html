About Us

Celebrating Two Decades of Excellence

Al Hazmi International is a name synonymous with cutting-edge industrial food equipment and packaging solutions in Saudi Arabia.

Celebrating two decades of excellence, Al Hazmi International is a name synonymous with cutting-edge industrial food equipment and packaging solutions in Saudi Arabia. Our journey over the last 20 years has been marked by a steadfast commitment to quality and innovation, catering to a diverse clientele that ranges from street food maestros and unique niche restaurants to high-end industrial bakeries, expert butcheries, Michelin-starred dining establishments, and luxurious five-star hotels and resorts.

Our approach is client-centric, always putting the needs and aspirations of our partners at the forefront. This philosophy has earned us a roster of reputed organizations, all testaments to our ability to deliver beyond expectations.

But our expertise doesn't end with the food industry. Al Hazmi International is also a robust solution provider for industrial racking and a trusted name in general trading for construction materials. Our breadth of services and products showcase our versatility and capability to meet various industrial needs.

As a proud division of Al Hazmi International, our specialized segment ensures that clients have access to the highest quality products from globally renowned brands. We offer comprehensive, end-to-end solutions for all catering equipment requirements. This includes meticulous design, sales, installation, and exceptional aftersales service, all supported by our team of highly trained service and maintenance catering engineers.

Whether our clients are in need of a single combination oven or a completely tailored cooking suite, Al Hazmi International stands ready to meet and exceed those needs with our expertise, dedication, and unparalleled service.
