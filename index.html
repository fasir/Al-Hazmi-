The Beginning Jeddah HQ Founded

2003 — Company Founded

Al Hazmi International was established with a vision to transform the industrial food equipment landscape in Saudi Arabia, starting with bakery equipment dealership and maintenance services. A bold dream was born in Jeddah.

Initial Growth 10+ Local Contracts

2005 — Market Expansion

Secured contracts with local bakeries and food establishments across Jeddah. Hired skilled technicians and began building a strong reputation for prompt, reliable service and equipment maintenance. Our client base grew steadily.

Official Incorporation Incorporated Entity

2009 — Company Establishment

Formally established as Al Hazmi International. Developed a comprehensive business plan, built strong supplier relationships with top bakery equipment manufacturers from around the world, and launched our full marketing strategy.

Scaling Up Expanded Vehicle Fleet

2010 — Expansion Phase

Expanded service coverage to larger geographic regions across the Kingdom. Diversified product offerings to include a wider range of bakery and food equipment, and invested in a professional fleet of service vehicles for on-site repairs and installation.

Digital Leap Diagnostics CMMS System

2015 — Technological Advancements

Implemented a computerized maintenance management system to streamline all operations. Explored innovative technologies for food equipment diagnostics and repair, and developed a strong online presence with website and social media to reach a wider audience.

Nationwide 5 Major KSA Cities

2017 — National Presence

Expanded operations nationally by opening branches in key cities across Saudi Arabia. Developed a robust customer loyalty program to retain existing clients and attract new partners, serving clients from niche restaurants to luxurious five-star hotels.

Innovation Research & Development

2019 — Research & Development

Allocated significant resources to research and develop new bakery equipment technologies. Explored franchising opportunities for rapid expansion across the Kingdom, and strengthened the brand's reputation through industry awards and international recognitions.

Global Reach UAE & Italy Branches

2023 — International Expansion

Expanded business to international markets in Italy and the UAE. Established a new kitchen equipment business division, diversified operations to include supermarket solutions, and firmly positioned Al Hazmi International as a leading global industrial partner.
