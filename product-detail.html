Overview

The Tagliavini ROTOR convection rack oven represents the pinnacle of industrial baking engineering. Engineered for high volume continuous output, it utilizes an alternating hot-air flow system combined with a rotating rack to ensure each tray is baked to golden perfection. Its thick mineral-wool insulation ensures thermal containment, keeping operational energy costs low.
